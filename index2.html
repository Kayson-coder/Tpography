THE HISTORY OF ELEMENTAL PLANET

Long before Earth knew the touch of life, the Elemental Planet was born in the heart of the cosmos, a world forged from the raw, untamed forces of creation itself. It was a realm where firestorms swept across crimson skies, glaciers sang as they moved like living giants, and floating mountains hovered effortlessly over oceans of liquid crystal. At its core pulsed the Elemental Core — a swirling nexus of pure power where fire, water, earth, air, light, and shadow existed in delicate, eternal balance. From this Core emerged the Elemental Ancients, towering beings of immense wisdom and strength, each shaped by an element they vowed to protect. To preserve their world’s harmony, they crafted the Elemental Stones — flawless gems infused with the primal forces of the universe — and paired each with a sacred weapon, placing them within colossal temples guarded by sentient golems. Over eons, civilizations rose around these temples: the Firewalkers built cities in blazing canyons, the Earthenborn carved homes in living mountains, the Windrunners soared among sky islands, and the Waterspeakers thrived in bioluminescent ocean domes. But as always, peace was fragile. Among the Ancients, Maltheron, once the guardian of gravity, was seduced by the hunger for dominion. Corrupting his power, he forged the Devil Stone, twisting gravity into a dark force that warped reality itself. His rebellion plunged the planet into a catastrophic war, shattering continents, boiling seas, and dimming the stars above. Desperate to save what remained, the Ancients sacrificed themselves, sealing Maltheron in a prison of collapsed dimensions and scattering the Elemental Stones across the universe to keep them from falling into mortal hands. Some fragments reached Earth, embedding themselves deep within its crust, hidden in temples and caves, waiting across millennia for the day they would awaken again. And now, as the balance trembles and ancient prophecies stir, a new generation is called — the Mighty Elemental Morphin Masters, destined to wield the power of a forgotten age.
